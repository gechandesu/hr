==
hr
==

------------------------------------
A horizontal ruler for your terminal
------------------------------------

:Author: ge
:Copyright: Unlicense
:Date: 2022 Jul 20
:Manual section: 1
:Version: baf 0.1.0

SYNOPSIS
========

hr <string>...

DESCRIPTION
===========

Print line with passed characters till the end of terminal window.

EXAMPLES
========

::

    $ hr '*'
    ***************************************

    $ hr '#--'
    #--#--#--#--#--#--#--#--#--#--#--#--#--

    $ hr + - +
    ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
    ----------------------------------------
    ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
